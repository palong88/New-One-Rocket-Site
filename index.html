The Perfect Onboarding Process

No more spreadsheets, emails and surveys. Save your HR team time and money by setting up an automated employee onboarding process with our easy-to-use dashboard.

  • Where to start?

    We've set up the perfect onboarding process for you. Simply tweak it using our templates to suit your needs.



  • Categories

    We've broken up the onboarding process into four separate categories; Paperwork, Apps & Tools, Meet the Company and Get Going.



  • Simple Setup

    Easily add employee tasks and documents to different categories. E.g. Add your employee handbook to the Paperwork category.



  • Employee Self-serve

    Employees get access to their own web app where they can complete tasks and view documents. You get notified when a task is overdue.

Set New Employees up for Success

A good onboarding process is critical to employee performance and retention. Give new employees everything they need to be successful from day one.

  • Before They Start

    Collect employee details and get paperwork out of the way so employees can hit the ground running on day one.



  • Their First Day

    Provide new employees with details about their first day, your company's culture and who they will be working with.



  • The next 90 days

    Easily organise one-to-ones and performance reviews with employees. Help them to have a successful career with your company.
